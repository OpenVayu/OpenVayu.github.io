Introduction to OpenVayu

OpenVayu is an initiative to bring together people from all across the board with a singular passion for making a change. We aim to research air quality and climate change and analyze how it affects our lifestyle and our planet, at large. Our vision is to mobilize thinkers and doers and to empower them for the betterment of our world.
